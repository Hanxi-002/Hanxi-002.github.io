I am a PhD candidate in the Joint CMU-Pitt PhD Program in Computational Biology, working in the Center for Systems Immunolog, advised by Jishnu Das.
My research interest lie in interpretable machine learning, statistical modeling, and their applications in high-dimentionsal noisy sparse tabular data and large-scale high-dimensional sequence data.
More specifically, my projects includes interpretable latent variable methods and interpretable attention mechanism.
